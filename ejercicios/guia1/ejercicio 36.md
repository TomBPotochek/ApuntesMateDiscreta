1 \leq (1+6x) \leq 84 \cdot (2+7)
$$

$$
\begin{array} \\
1 \leq (1 +6x) \\

\end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array} \\
(1+6x) \leq 84 \cdot 14 \\
6x \leq 14 \\
